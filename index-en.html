Khamid and Zulfia

Khamid and Zulfia

The documentary covers the work and love of the outstanding Uzbek poets Hamid Alimzhan and Zulfiya, and the poetess' life after tragic demise of her beloved. Authors' poems are read by the most famous performers of Uzbekistan: Gavkhar Zakirova, Sevara Nazarkhan, Rustam Sagdullaev, Adiz Radjabov, Yulduz Radjabova and others.

Treasures of maqom and Uzbek traditional music

new vision

A classic album of 15 tracks, recorded via Multitrack technology. More than 50 people took part in the recording process. Classic traditional music performed by 'Makom' ensemble led by the People's Artist of Uzbekistan Abdukhoshim Ismoilov.

The tracks of 'Treasures of Maqom' were used at the celebrations of Independence Day of Uzbekistan multiple times.

Uzbek Dance Legends

Legends of Uzbek dance

A documentary about the history of national choreographic art and important figures who stand at its origins. The documentary covers modern and traditional national dances representing Fergana, Bukhara, Khorezm and other regions of Uzbekistan.

sadoqat

A short musical film based on Abdulla Kadiri's 'Past Days', performed by the masters of traditional music Munojat Yulchiyeva, Abdukhoshim Ismoilov, Tahir Askar and others. The text is read by the People's Artist of Uzbekistan Afzal Rafikov.
